Chubba
PROJECT:
Chubba (WIP)

ROLES:
Type Design
Take a sans-serif typeface and pump it chock-full of air. That was the idea behind Chubba, a rounded display sans that appears to be so full and chunky it overlaps itself. Instead of having a traditional weight axis, Chubba has a variable optical-size axis to maintain legibility even at small sizes. This main character set is currently still in production, with further numerals, symbols and punctuation coming soon.

Current Version: v.0.1.b
