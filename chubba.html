Chubba (WIP)

Tons of cushion.

Type Design

Take any regular sans and pump it chock-full of air. That was the idea behind Chubba, a rounded display sans that appears to be so thick it overlaps itself. Instead of having a traditional weight axis, Chubba has an optical axis (variable) to maintain legibility even at small sizes. This Typeface is currently still in production.

Current Version: v.001 Beta
