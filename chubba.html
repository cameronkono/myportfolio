Chubba
Chubba
Categories:

Type Design
Take a sans-serif typeface and pump it chock-full of air. That was the idea behind Chubba, a rounded display sans that appears to be so thick and chunky it overlaps itself. Instead of having a traditional weight axis, Chubba has a variable optical-size axis to maintain legibility even at small sizes. This typeface is currently still in production. Numerals and more punctuation coming soon.

Current Version: v.0.1 Beta
