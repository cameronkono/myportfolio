Zelus is a brand new social fitness app that connects friends and athletes from across the world. With Zelus, users can participate in real-time running and cycling races against global competitors, in private races with friends, or in charity races for a cause of their choosing. The team at Zelus reached out to me to create a brand new logo and visual identity for their iOS and Android App.

↳ I. Logo

Zelus gets its name from Zelos, a daemon from Greek mythology that personifies dedication, competition and zeal. A winged foot in motion became the main concept for the logomark, inspired by the wings on the back of the daemon Zelos.

↳ II. Typography

Zelus' typography references the condesned bold type that is frequently found on classic race bibs and cards, used to identify competitors in races. Dharma Gothic (Ryochi Tsunekawa, 2011) is inspired by historic wood type and perfectly embodies this style with its tall and loud letterforms. Aktiv Grotesk (Dalton Maag, 2010) is a flexible, neutral neo-grotesque and our supporting typeface.
