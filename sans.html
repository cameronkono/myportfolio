PROJECT:
Flatty v.1.01 (beta)

ROLES:
Type Design
Flatty is my personal study of the swiss neo-grotesque classics drawn by the likes of Walter Kach, Karl Gerstner and Max Miedigner, just to name a few. This typeface takes inspiration from the neutral and rational character of Swiss neo-grotesque typefaces, while retaining a certain amount of warmth due to its more geometric influences. This typeface is still a work in progress, with more weights, kerning and italics coming soon.

This entire site is set in this typeface.

Particular influences include:
(•) Neue Haas Unica (Team 77, 1980)
(•) Programm (Karl Gerstner, 1964)
(•) Neue Haas Grotesk (Eduard Hoffmann & Max Miedingner, 1957)
(•) Rauchwaren (Walter Kach, 1949)
(•) Univers (Adrian Frutiger, 1957)
