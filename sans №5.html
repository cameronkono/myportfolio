Sans №5

Explorations of the neo-grotesque genre.

Type Design

Sans №5 is a neo-grotesque I designed to meet modern requirements of a sans-typeface and for use in my own applications. This typeface takes inspiration from the neutrality and rationality of Swiss neo-grotesque typefaces, while retaining a warmth reminiscent of the geometric sans genre. This typeface is still a work in progress, with more weights, axes and italics coming soon.

Current Version: v.004
