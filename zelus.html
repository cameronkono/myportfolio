Zelus


Logo & Identity
Type Design

Zelus is a brand new social fitness app, connecting friends and athletes from across the globe. With Zelus, users can participate in real-time running and cycling races against global competitors, or in private races with friends.

The team at Zelus reached out to me to create a brand new visual idenity for their upcoming iOS & Android App.

Logo Construction

The App gets its name from Zelos, a daimon from Greek mythology that personifies dedication, competition and zeal. A winged foot became the main concept for the brand-mark, inspired by the wings of the daimon.

Zelus Titling

Zelus uses a modified version of Archivo (orginally designed by Hector Gatti of Omnibus Type) which has been distilled into two text cuts (Regular & Bold) and one titling cut.

Changes include a sharply jointed /Z, an alternate /Y, a spur-less /a, round dots & tittles, slightly exaggerated terminals, higher tapering in the titling cut, as well as other changes.
