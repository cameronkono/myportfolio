CLIENT:
Zelus

ROLES:
Identity, Logo, UX/UI, 3D

Zelus is a brand new social fitness app that connects friends and athletes from across the world. With Zelus, users can participate in real-time running and cycling races against global competitors, in private races with friends, or in charity races for a cause of their choosing. The team at Zelus reached out to me to create a brand new logo and visual identity for their iOS and Android App.

Zelus gets its name from the Greek mythological daemon Zelos. Zelos, brother to Nike, Bia and Kratos, personifies dedication, competition and zeal. A winged foot in motion became the main concept for the logomark, inspired by the wings found on the back of the daemon Zelos.

Zelus’ typography references the condensed bold type that is frequently found on classic race bibs and cards, used to identify competitors in races. Dharma Gothic (Ryochi Tsunekawa, 2011) perfectly embodies this style with its loud and slender letterforms and functions as Zelus' display typeface. Aktiv Grotesk (Dalton Maag, 2010), a flexible and neutral neo-grotesque, is used as Zelus' supporting typeface.
