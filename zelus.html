Zelus

Building a new social fitness experience.

Logo
Identity
UI/UX

Zelus is a brand new social fitness app that connects friends and athletes from across the globe. With Zelus, users can participate in real-time running and cycling races against global competitors, in private races with friends or in charity races for a cause of their choosing.

The team at Zelus reached out to me to create a brand new visual identity for their upcoming iOS and Android App.

Logomark Construction

Zelus gets its name from Zelos, a daemon from Greek mythology that personifies dedication, competition and zeal. A winged foot in motion became the main concept for the logomark, inspired by the wings on the back of the daemon Zelos.

Typography

Zelus’ typography is inspired by the condesned bold type that is frequently found on race bibs and cards. Rama Gothic, inspired by historic wood type, perfectly embodies this style with its tall and loud letterforms. Roc Grotesk, which is also inspired by historic wood type, is used as a secondary supporting typeface.
