Amorous

Walls of Sound.

Illustration
Packaging

Amorous is the musical project of Alec Young, based in Washington DC. Velvety distortion and reverb-drenched vocals conjure a daydream-like quality. After playing together in another musical project, Alec reached out to create an alubm cover for his next solo album, Sitting Pretty.

Through a mix of phsyical 'filters', like a faceted glass lens and photocopy warping, a photo of his back lawn (where Alec likes to write music in good weather) planter was altered to achieve a cover that was uniquely Amorous — heavily layered, organic and noise-drenched. A dark red was chosen as the accent and cassette color, matching Alec's 1966 Candy Apple Red Jaguar, the main tool used to create his sound.
