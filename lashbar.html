Lashbar

Reimagining tailored beauty.

Logomark
Identity

Lashbar is a chain of salons around Southern California, specializing in lash extensions and other lash services. With an empahsis on client-first service, Lashbar technicians ensure that every service is bespoke to the client's unique preferences. While working with Lashbar stakeholders, I devised revisions to the brand, such as revamping the logomark, typography and more.

Redrawn Logomark

At lashbar, technicians are also called 'Lash Faries' — because they make the magic happen. The old logomark symbolized this concept, but it was complicated and lost legibility and clarity at large sizes. Though the logotype remains unchanged (the stakeholders' descision), the new logomark has been simplified and refined to work in any contextow m while still paying homage to the Lash Fairies that make everything possible.

Typograhpic Specs

Just like it's service, Lashbar's typograhpy aims to be warm, professional and elegant. Freight Big is used for larger, eye-catching headings, while Sofia Pro is used for small headings and body copy.
